switchView

a simple task manager to demonstrate my implementation of the NgRx store

Why this app?

This is app is developed for my Front-End Engineer application & candidacy at ClickUp. The NgRx Store (aka Redux Pattern) is something I’ve understood conceptually, but personally never had to implement.

My hope is this app will give validity to my abilities.
